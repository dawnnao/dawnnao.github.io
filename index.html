About Me

My research studies structural health monitoring (SHM) for civil infrastructures. To meet the practical demand from large-scale multimodal SHM data processing, I am currently interested in developing computationally efficient and statistical guaranteed methods that learn structural behavior and performance under modern deep learning architectures.

I am a fourth year Ph.D. student at HIT working with Prof. Yuequan Bao. I completed my bachelor degree and master degree here as well, advised by Prof. Hui Li. Going to a HIT cubic!
